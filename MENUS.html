Starters

Dipping, rolling and sharing are what our starters and sides were made for. We make everything fresh in-house, from our summer and spring rolls to our pork and lemongrass meatballs. All come with their own sauces, but you can also try them with our delicious table condiments: homemade chilli paste, garlic vinegar, sriracha or fish sauce.

Summer rolls

Fresh rice paper summer rolls with vermicelli & pickle. Veggie, chicken, prawn, or vegan chicken

SPRING ROLLS

Crispy spring rolls served with lettuce & herbs to wrap & dip. Veggie or pork.

SEAFOOD SPRING ROLL

Large crispy spring roll of tiger prawn, crab & pork with nước chấm dipping sauce

SPICY SALAD ROLLS

spicy salad rolls with enoki, chilli & herbs (carb free)

CHICKEN WINGS

Seasoned, crispy chicken wings served with sriracha

VIETNAMESE PANCAKE

Savoury Vietnamese crispy crepe with rice papers & herbs. Chicken & prawn, tofu or vegan chicken

MORNING GLORY

Stir fried morning glory (water spinach) in garlic

BEEF BETEL

Beef wrapped in betel leaves, served with rice vermicelli sheets & nuoc cham

Salads

Crispy, colourful, tasty and filling, our delicious spicy Vietnamese salads are light years away from mundane lettuce-based salads.

CHICKEN SALAD

Shredded chicken salad with Vietnamese herbs, peppers and a chilli ginger dressing

VEGGIE SALAD

Same as Goi Ga, minus the chicken, or you can add in vegan THIS isn't chicken

MANGO SALAD

Spicy green mango salad topped with pork, dried shrimp & peanuts

GREEN PAPAYA SALAD

Crunchy green papaya salad with peanuts & prawn crackers. Veggie, prawn or vegan THIS isn't chicken

PHO

Phở, pronounced ‘fuh,’ is the national dish of Vietnam. It’s a fantastic steaming bowl of rice noodle soup served with a side plate of fresh herbs to add as you please.

Phở đặc biệt

King prawns, chicken & flash fried steak with garlic in beef stock

Phở chay

Tofu & button mushrooms in veggie broth

Phở bò

Thinly sliced steak in beef broth

Phở gà

Chicken breast in chicken broth

Bún riêu

A Hanoi classic; vermicelli noodles in a rich tomato & crab broth with wafer thin steak

BEEF COMBO PHỞ

Steak, brisket & meatballs in beef broth
